Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 25, 2026 Version 1.0

ClearOut (the "Application") is built and engineered as a 100% local-first utilities application. We highly value your personal privacy and are committed to safeguarding all user data. This privacy document details how our application handles information, permissions, and device storage.

"At ClearOut, we believe your personal photos should remain personal. The application indexes and processes your media purely on your device, requiring zero network calls or remote data uploads."

1. Data Collection & Transmission

We do NOT collect, harvest, store, or transmit any of your personal data, photos, metadata, or telemetry metrics. All photo indexing, gallery binning, deletion queuing, and stats aggregation operations are computed locally in-memory on your device using Android's native secure MediaStore Scoped Storage APIs. Your media never leaves your device.

2. Permissions Requested

To deliver a smooth utility experience, the Application requests only the narrowest possible permissions:

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Solely utilized to load image cataloging details (URIs, file dimensions, file sizes, and date stamps) in order to render them inside the Tinder-style swipe cards interface.
  • VIBRATE: Used to trigger standard, satisfying haptic clicks synchronizing with swipe physical motions.

3. Third-Party Libraries & Telemetry

The Application does not include any third-party background trackers, analytical scripts (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics), or target advertisement networks. We do not track user behavior or usage telemetry.

4. Data Reset & Control

All accumulated user progress metrics (e.g., photos cleared, MBs freed, and streak counters) are saved locally within your device's Jetpack Preferences DataStore. You can completely and permanently delete this telemetry at any time by selecting the Reset Stats action inside the in-app overflow menu (`⋮`). Doing so clears all local caches instantly.

5. Contact & Support Information

If you have any questions, suggestions, or policy feedback, please feel free to reach out to us at: support@clearout.app.
